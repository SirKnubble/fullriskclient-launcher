# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report vulnerabilities at [discord.gg/noriskclient](https://discord.gg/noriskclient).

Read their [Security Policy](https://github.com/NoRiskClient/issues/security/policy), and maybe consider creating an [Issue](https://github.com/NoRiskClient/issues/issues/new?template=security_report.yml).

If they won´t answer, or I am the vulnerability, contact me at any time on Discord: [@sirknubble](https://discord.com/users/473390696192016386)
